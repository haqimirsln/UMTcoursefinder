Why UMT?

Malaysia's premier maritime university

Nestled on the shores of the South China Sea in Kuala Nerus, Terengganu, UMT offers a one-of-a-kind coastal campus experience with world-class research facilities.

Coastal Campus

Situated right by the South China Sea, unique for hands-on marine science exposure.

Active Research Culture

Multiple research institutes, from oceanography to marine technology and biodiversity.

Industry Connections

Strong ties with maritime industries, government agencies, and conservation organisations.
