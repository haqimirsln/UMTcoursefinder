Why UMT?

Malaysia's premier
maritime university

Nestled on the shores of the South China Sea in Kuala Nerus, Terengganu, UMT offers a one-of-a-kind coastal campus with world-class research facilities across 8 faculties.

Coastal Campus
Right by the South China Sea — unique hands-on marine science exposure for students.
Active Research Culture
Multiple institutes spanning oceanography, marine technology, biodiversity, and blue economy.
Industry Connections
Strong partnerships with maritime industries, government agencies, and conservation organisations.
